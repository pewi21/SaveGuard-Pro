Features

Everything you need to
protect your work

A complete safety net for your creative workflow, with intelligent automation that stays out of your way.

⚡

AutoSave

Automatically saves your document whenever changes are detected. Set the check interval and forget about manual Ctrl+S.

🧠

Smart Delay

Dynamically adjusts the save interval based on actual save duration, keeping Photoshop responsive on heavy files.

🗄️

AutoBackup

Creates timestamped backup copies at a separate interval. Never lose a version again, even after destructive edits.

🎯

Cohesion Delay

Waits for a pause in your editing activity before triggering a save, so saves never interrupt your creative flow.

📁

Multiple Formats

Save and back up as PSD, PSB, PNG, or JPG with per-format settings including JPG quality and PNG interlacing.

🔒

Crash-safe Storage

Settings written atomically with .tmp/.bak recovery. Your configuration is safe even if Photoshop crashes mid-save.

📄

Per-document Settings

Each open document has its own independent configuration. Different workflows for different files, simultaneously.

🚫

Ignored Operations

Define lists of Photoshop history states (like Lasso or Move Selection) that don't trigger a save. No junk backups.

🌍

11 Languages

Available in EN, PL, DE, FR, ES, IT, PT, RU, ZH, JA, KO. The whole team can use it comfortably.

Installation

Up and running
in under 2 minutes

1

Open Adobe Photoshop

Make sure you have Photoshop 23.3.0 or later installed on Windows or macOS.

2

Go to Plugins → Browse Plugins

Or visit Adobe Exchange directly in your browser and search for SaveGuard Pro.

3

Click Get and follow the prompts

The plugin installs automatically. Your 30-day trial starts immediately, no credit card required.

4

Open via Plugins → SaveGuard Pro

The panel opens inside Photoshop. Enable AutoSave and you're protected from this moment on.
